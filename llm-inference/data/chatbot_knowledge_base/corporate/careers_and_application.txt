Gleneagles Hospital Penang - Careers and How to Apply

Join our team of dedicated professionals making a difference in healthcare every day. We offer roles in:
- Clinical (Doctors, Nurses, Allied Health)
- Non-clinical (Administration, Operations, Finance, IT)

How to apply:
- Visit: www.gleneagles.com.my/penang/careers
- Submit your CV and cover letter through the online portal
- For inquiries: careers@gleneagles-penang.com

Internships and training programs are also available for students in healthcare-related fields.
